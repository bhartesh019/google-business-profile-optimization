
# Google Business Profile (GMB) Optimization for Local SEO

## Project Overview

This project was completed as part of a Digital Marketing Internship. The objective was to optimize a Google Business Profile (formerly Google My Business) to improve local search visibility using Local SEO techniques.

## Business Details

- **Business Name:** Shree Hari Collection
- **Business Category:** Clothing Store
- **Location:** Gurbax Colony Main Rd, Opposite Street No. 8, Guru Nanak Nagar, Patiala, Punjab – 147001

## Project Objectives

- Create and optimize a Google Business Profile.
- Perform keyword research for local SEO.
- Optimize the business description.
- Add business services and photos.
- Build local citations with consistent NAP (Name, Address, Phone Number).
- Analyze Google Business Profile performance.

## Work Completed

- Business profile created and verified.
- Business information updated.
- Local keyword research performed.
- SEO-friendly business description added.
- Business category and services optimized.
- Business photos uploaded.
- Local citation strategy prepared.
- Google Business Profile link generated.

## Challenges Faced

The Google Business Profile was successfully verified. However, the business phone number and WhatsApp chat URL were marked as "Not Approved" by Google's automated review system. The issue was investigated and support resources were consulted.
